About Us

Bilkent Game Development & Design Club is dedicated to fostering creativity and collaboration among students passionate about game development. We provide an engaging environment where members can explore game design, development, and storytelling, using popular engines like Unity and Unreal Engine.

Upcoming Events

We organize various activities throughout the year, including workshops, game jams, and guest speaker sessions with industry professionals.

  • Game Development Workshop: Learn the fundamentals of game development with Unity. (Every Saturday, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)
  • Game Jam Competition: Join our 48-hour team-based game-making challenge! (Once per semester)
  • Guest Talk: Engage with professional game designers and industry experts. (Once a month)

Our Projects

Our members have worked on diverse game projects, from pixel art platformers to complex RPGs. Each project is an opportunity for members to learn and collaborate in a dynamic, hands-on environment.
